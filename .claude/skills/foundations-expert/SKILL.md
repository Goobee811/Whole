## Expert Tips

1. **Grouping Strategy**: In FOUNDATIONS, group by *philosophical tradition* or *conceptual theme*, not by application domain

2. **Abstraction Level**: FOUNDATIONS concepts should be abstract and timeless - avoid specific technologies or modern frameworks

3. **Bilingual Balance**: FOUNDATIONS has many Eastern concepts - ensure Vietnamese terms are culturally authentic, not just translations

4. **Cross-Domain Links**: FOUNDATIONS concepts link to MANY other domains - maintain these carefully
